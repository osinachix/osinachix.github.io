"Can AI Doctors Be Fair? Unpacking the Hidden Gender Bias in Large Language Models"
Shao, Z., Adjekwei, J. A., Safarpoor, M., Mbakamma, O.E.I.
Micropublications - Neuromatch Academy Impact Scholar Program, 2025

Abstract: This research investigates gender bias in clinical AI systems by comparing domain-specific clinical LLMs with general-domain BERT models. We examine how both inherit and process gender biases from medical data, with findings showing reduced gender bias in specialized clinical LLMs for symptom-related predictions.

"Machine Learning-Based Optimization of Wireless Charging for Mobile Robot Platforms"
Mbakamma, O.
Neuromatch Academy Deep Learning Conference, 2024

Abstract: Presents a novel framework leveraging CNNs, RNNs, GAs, and RL to optimize wireless power transfer systems. The research demonstrates 18-22% reduction in energy loss and 21% decrease in charging time through comprehensive simulation and validation.
