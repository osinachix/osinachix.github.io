PhD Research Directions

Primary: Trustworthy Embedded AI Systems

Primary Research Focus Embedded AI, Security, Optimization

Research Questions:

  • How can we ensure adversarial robustness in resource-constrained AI systems with limited computational budgets?
  • What architectural patterns and training methodologies enable privacy-preserving edge intelligence while maintaining performance?
  • How to achieve real-time inference guarantees while maintaining security and safety constraints in autonomous systems?
  • What are the fundamental trade-offs between model complexity, security guarantees, and energy efficiency in embedded AI?
